ABOUT US:

Bubbles Celebration Co. is a small South African business that sells birthday packages, gift hampers and other great birthday celebration items. these packages include items like cakes, balloons,small gifts, party decorations and personal messages. people can order online and get them delivered.

We believe that every special moment deserves to be celebrated in a unique way. Our team works with love and creativity to design hampers and packages that bring smiles to people of all ages. We are passionate about making every birthday, anniversary or surprise extra memorable. We also offer personalised packages so that every customer can create something unique.

We believe in quality, creativity and excellent customer service. Whether it’s for a child, a teenager or an adult, our goal is to make birthdays easy, fun and unforgettable.
